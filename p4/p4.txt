FizzBuzz
Write a program that prints the numbers from 1 to n, but for multiples of 3 print "Fizz" instead of the number, and for the multiples of 5 print "Buzz". For numbers which are multiples of both three and five, print "FizzBuzz".

Input
A single integer n.

Output
Print the numbers from 1 to n, each on a new line, with the appropriate substitutions for multiples of 3, 5, or both.
